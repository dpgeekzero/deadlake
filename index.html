Dead Lake Scrolls

I got my first scrollsaw in 2010 and since then I can't think of anything I'd rather be doing. I make what is called “scrollsaw art” which includes both wall plaques free standing pieces.

My art follows some common themes:

  • Pictures
  • Words of Wisdom
  • Southwest scenes
  • Christian religious symbols and sayings
  • Holiday related, especially Christmas ornaments
  • Whimsical
  • Animals

    • I cut these from varying thicknesses of wood, some plywood and some hardwood, from 1/32in to 3/4in. The intricate designs on many of the pieces are called fretwork. Some require a spiral blade that can cut in any direction, others a blade not much thicker than a human hair.
